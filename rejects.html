Welcome to the Rejects collection, where you'll find some of the most powerful and unique NFTs on Ethereum. These player characters were originally rejected from our flagship collection, MiladyStation, due to their extraordinary abilities and otherworldly qualities. But now, we're releasing them into the world for free, exclusively for our holders.

The Rejects collection is built on the ERC1155 standard, which means that each NFT is a unique and indivisible asset that can be owned and traded on the Ethereum blockchain. We've also designed the minting process to be a "mint your own" experience, which means that you'll be able to create your own NFTs with custom metadata and properties. This gives you full control over your collection and allows you to express your creativity in unique ways.

However, we must warn you that owning a Reject is not for the faint of heart. These fallen angels are powerful and mysterious, and they require a certain level of responsibility and ownership from their owners. You'll need to take care of them, protect them, and make sure they're used for good purposes. But if you're up for the challenge, the Rejects collection is waiting for you.
